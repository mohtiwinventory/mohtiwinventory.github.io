How to Use the Damage Report System

For Students & Staff

The **Damage Report System** allows you to report damaged items in the school.

Follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Report an Issue" button in your dashboard.
  2. A pop-up form will appear.
  3. Fill in the required details:
    • Object: Name of the damaged item.
    • Location: Where the item is located.
    • Status: Select whether it is Minor Damage, Major Damage, or Needs Immediate Repair.
    • Date: When the damage was noticed.
  4. Click **Submit**. The report will be sent to the **admin** for review.

For Admins

The **Admin Panel** allows you to view, resolve, and manage reports.

Steps to manage reports:

  1. Go to the Admin Reports page.
  2. You will see a **list of reported damages** in a table.
  3. Each report has two actions:
    • Resolve: Marks the issue as resolved and removes it from the list.
    • Delete: Removes the report from the system.

FAQs

Q: Can I edit my report after submitting?

A: No, but you can contact the admin if changes are needed.

Q: Will I be notified when my report is resolved?

A: Not yet, but future updates may include email notifications.
